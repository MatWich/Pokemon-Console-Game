Supported purposes

Damage:

damageDeal - deals damage to the enemy.

Debuffs:

  • ATK_Debuff - reduces enemy's attack.
  • DEF_Debuff - reduces enemy's defence.
  • SATK_debuff - reduces enemy's special attack.
  • SDEF_Debuff - reduces enemy's special defence.
  • SPD_Debuff - reduces enemy's speed

Buffs:

  • ATK_Buff - increases attack.
  • DEF_Buff - increases defence.
  • SATK_Buff - increases special attack.
  • SDEF_Buff - increases special defence.
  • SPD_Buff - increases speed.
  • HealUp - increases your's pokemon lifepoint (cannot restore more than pokemon.maxHp).
